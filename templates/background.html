A.I trash sorter: A step towards a cleaner future.

Json, Jun Yi, Jetro aka ChatGPT Abusers
Made for Tryhack

Recycling is able to save the world, but it depends on our help. Often, a recycling bin receives the wrong item in their bins, materials not of their classification. This is usually remedied by sorting factories in China, which requires many low-paid workers to sort thousands of filthy waste in an assembly line to ensure that what is left is waste that can be converted to raw material.

The Solution

With the latest AI technology, it liberates these workers from labor intensive jobs. That is why we decided to create an AI that can sort garbage into certain categories for waste management.

The A.I uses computer vision to deduce from the image the percentage propability of what the material could be. With this, other machines can also appropriately sort the garbage into certain materials with less human help. And since A.I can work 24/7, it is also cheaper and faster.
